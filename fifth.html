And you really will have to make it through that violent,
metaphysical,
symbolic storm.
No matter how metaphysical or symbolic it might be,
make no mistake about it:
it will cut through flesh
like a thousand razor blades.
People will bleed there,
and you will bleed too; hot, red blood.
You’ll catch that blood in your hands,
your own blood and the blood of others.

And once the storm is over you won’t remember
how you made it through,
how you managed to survive.
You won’t even be sure,
in fact, whether the storm is really over.
